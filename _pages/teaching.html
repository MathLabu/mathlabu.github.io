My teaching activities topics can be organized in four categeries: Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Control and Automation, and Mathematics. I teached both undergrate and gradute classes, from three different composantes (IUT GIM, ISIMA, and EUPI).

As an Associate Professor

Since September 2022, I am giving classes at the IUT Clermont Auvergne, in the Industrial Engineering and Maintenance department (GIM).

Details year by year are provided below!

As a Temporary Teaching and Research Associate (ATER)

From January to June 2022, I did my teaching activities as part of my ATER contract in the University School of Physics and Engineering (EUPI) at the Université Clermont Auvergne (UCA).

I accomplished a teaching service of 96.5 hours ETD composed of lectures coupled with tutorials (38.5 hours) and practical work (82.5 hours). I intervened in two Masters, the Master Automatique-Robotique (AR), and the Master Traitement du Signal et des Images (TSI), in first and second years on several courses.

I was in charge of the Robotics and Vision course, and of the Introduction to ROS software module.

As a contractual PhD student

During my doctoral degree, I accomplished a teaching service of 187.5 hours ETD composed of lectures (40 hours), tutorials (23.5 hours) and practical work (124 hours), within the components of the Institut Supérieur d'Informatique, de Modélisation et de leurs Applications (ISIMA, now part of the INP Clermont Auvergne) and of the IUT Clermont Auvergne, Industrial Engineering and Maintenance department (GIM).

I intervened in the 1st year of the DUT GIM, and on the three years of the training of engineer in Computer Sciences and Modeling of the ISIMA and in particular on the last two years specialized in informatics of embedded systems.

I was responsible for the course of Integration of sensors for robotics.
